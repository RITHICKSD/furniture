Our Story

15 years of passion, precision, and a love for beautiful spaces.

Our Mission

Crafting Spaces That Feel Like Home

Founded in 2010 in Copenhagen, Furnique was born from a simple belief: every home deserves furniture that tells its owner's unique story. We partner with master craftsmen across Scandinavia and Southeast Asia to bring you pieces that are as durable as they are beautiful.

Today, we serve over 12,000 families across 30 countries — each one with a home they're proud to show off.
