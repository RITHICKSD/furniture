What We Offer

End-to-end furniture experiences — from design to your doorstep.

Our Services

Everything You Need

We handle every step of the journey so you can focus on loving your home.

Interior Design Consultation

Our expert designers work one-on-one with you to craft a cohesive room layout and furniture selection tailored to your lifestyle and budget.

Custom Furniture Orders

Choose from 40+ fabric options, dozens of wood finishes, and custom dimensions. Every piece is made to order, made to fit.

White-Glove Delivery

Our professional delivery team delivers, assembles, and places every item exactly where you want it — and removes all packaging.

30-Day Returns

Not completely satisfied? Return any item within 30 days for a full refund — no questions asked, no hassle.

10-Year Warranty

Every Furnique piece comes with a decade-long structural warranty. We build for life, not for landfill.

Repair & Restoration

We offer professional repair and refinishing services to keep your furniture looking its best for generations.
