What We Offer

End-to-end furniture experiences — from design to your doorstep.

Our Services

Everything You Need

We handle every step of the journey so you can focus on loving your home.

Personalized Interior Design

Our award-winning designers provide comprehensive 1-on-1 consultations. We create high-fidelity 3D renderings and spatial plans to help you visualize exactly how our pieces will transform your specific living environment before you buy.

Bespoke Customization

Every piece is a canvas. We offer over 200 premium fabric choices, 15 sustainably sourced wood finishes, and the ability to adjust dimensions down to the centimeter, ensuring your furniture fits your space and style with surgical precision.

Concierge White-Glove Delivery

Our dedicated logistics team doesn't just drop off boxes. We handle full assembly, precise room placement, and comprehensive packaging removal. We treat your home with the same care we treat our craftsmanship.

Confidence Guarantee

We stand by our quality. If a piece doesn't feel right in your home, our 30-day "No-Questions" return policy ensures a seamless collection and full refund process. Your peace of mind is our highest priority.

Lifetime Heritage Warranty

Our 10-year structural warranty is a promise of longevity. We build furniture to become family heirlooms, using joinery techniques that have stood the test of centuries to ensure your investment lasts a lifetime.

Artisan Restoration

True luxury matures. Our master restorers offer exclusive refinishing and upholstery services for Furnique owners, ensuring that even after decades of use, your cherished pieces can be restored to their original brilliance.
